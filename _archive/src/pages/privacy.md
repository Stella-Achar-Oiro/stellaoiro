## Cookies

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They help us improve your experience and understand how you use our Site.

### Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies**
- Required for site functionality
- Remember your preferences
- Cannot be disabled

**Analytics Cookies**
- Help us understand site usage
- Track page views and user behavior
- Can be disabled in your browser

**Advertising Cookies** (if applicable)
- Used for affiliate marketing
- Track conversions from affiliate links
- Can be disabled in your browser

### Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- [Chrome Cookie Settings](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox Cookie Settings](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari Cookie Settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)

Note: Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.
